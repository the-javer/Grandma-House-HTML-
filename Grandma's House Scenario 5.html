Grandma's House

Right before you walk over to your grandma you look at

your doorway and she is standing there already.

She offers you cookies but before you respond to her question

you ask her your question about the smell. Her face loses all

emotion and then you feel something poke your arm. You see a small

syrince that has a pale blue liquid in it. You get dizzy and fall to

the ground and right before you close your eyes you hear your grandma say

"good thing I kept that cyanide".

You died.
